errata and notes

A Cryptic Quintuplet
DHH are Dylan Hendrickson's initials. Clue 2 references Jason, whose initials are JY/JSON. Some clues must be read from the perspective of someone living at ET. The final step requires being physically present at ET.

The ET Parity Test
Some of this puzzle is impossible without knowledge about the members of ET.

Untangle My Spaghetti!
This puzzle operates according to 0.17 splitter behavior, but ignore the distinction between lanes (i.e. splitters will always output right, left, right, left, etc. regardless of the input lane).

Battery
The solution is not unique. :(

Minionry
The bottom left chess subpuzzle has a flaw that makes the intended solution not work. Minions of Darkness is an ET-created board game with the important properties that a.) unit N is designed to counter units N-2, N-1, and N+3; and b.) all units move simultaneously on a player's turn.
